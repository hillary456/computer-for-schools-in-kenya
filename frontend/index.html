About Computer for Schools Kenya

We are a non-profit organization dedicated to transforming donated computers into powerful learning tools for Kenyan schools. Since our inception, we've been committed to bridging the digital divide by extending the useful life of technology through expert refurbishment.

Our Mission

To bridge the digital divide in Kenya by providing high-quality refurbished computers to schools and enhancing digital literacy.

Community Impact

Empowering students and teachers with technology while creating sustainable employment opportunities.

Sustainability

Promoting environmental care by extending the lifespan of electronic devices through repair and upgrades.

Quality Assurance

Every refurbished computer meets our strict quality standards before reaching schools.

Transforming Lives Through Technology

Every donated computer has the potential to change a student's life. We carefully refurbish each device, install educational software, and ensure it reaches schools that need it most.
